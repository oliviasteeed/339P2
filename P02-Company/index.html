welcome to your new favourite website

Fancyplants is a place to buy, sell and exchange houseplants from around the world. Created by one crazy plant lady with too much time (and plants) on her hands, Fancyplants provides a meeting place for global houseplant lovers to share the calming properties of houseplants at an affordable price.

how it works

buy

Purchase unique, rare and beautiful houseplants for a fraction of market price.

sell

Add cuttings and plants to your shelf, allowing other planters to make offers and purchase them.

exchange

Message fellow planters to offer exchanges and gain more plant varieties in a win-win scenario.

learn

Join the community of plant lovers and learn about plant care, propagation and more.
