brand identity

goals

FANCYPLANTS is a website allowing users to buy, sell and exchange houseplants with users around the world, allowing more people to enjoy the beauty and mental benefits of plant care.

identity

FANCYPLANTS conveys a calm, youthful, creative and quirky attitude. They aim to appeal to Gen Z and millennial consumers of houseplants who are comfortable using online peer-to-peer resale marketplaces like Poshmark and Depop. Brand messaging is playful and realistic, as if talking to a real person. FANCYPLANTS focuses both on the aesthetic and collection value of plants as well as the therapeutic mental health benefits plants can provide.

benefits

FancyPlants offers more options than a garden centre, and the convenience of having plants shipped to one’s door, plus better prices. It also affords exchanging and selling one’s own plant clippings, which would typically otherwise be composted. Lastly, FancyPlants facilitates entrance into a community of similar houseplant lovers to connect with and learn from.

visual identity

FANCYPLANTS

Our logo uses Pilowlava, an eccentric font that reflects the rounded nodules of houseplant growth, and funky, fun brand aesthetic.

Headings are GT Super Display.

Headings are set in GT Super Display, a unique serif font relfecting the beauty and elegance of plants.

Subheadings use Alte Haas Grotesk.

Subheadings use Alte Haas Grotesk, a rounded version of the classic sans serif Neue Haas Grotesk, that features rounded corners for a vintage appeal. The sans serif serves the utilitarian goal of infromative and navigational subheadings and contrasts clearly with the serifed title font, plus provides legibility at a smaller size.

Body copy uses Alte Haas Grotesk too!

Body copy is written in Alte Haas Grotesk as well for visual clarity and cohesion.

Brand imagery is user-created, so will not be uniform. Site-selected images focus on vivid colours of plants with neutral but funky bedroom and house backgrounds.
